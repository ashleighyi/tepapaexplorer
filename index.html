How does Te Papa Explorer work?

We want to connect you with our musesum and exhibits,
making it easier to discover what you love and interests you.

  • Take and Share Photos

    Take photos of your favourite pieces in an exhibtion and share it with other Te Papa Explorers!

  • Find your interests

    Select your interests and get regular notifications or emails with new events and exhibitions that we think you would enjoy!

  • Learn more

    With this interactive app, you will be able to participate in quizzes and learn more facts about the exhibition you are at

  • Watch

    Watch videos about artists and artwork that are exculsive to the Te Papa Explorer App

  • Review

    Write and share mini reviews on specific exhibitions, share on social media and read reviews from other Te Papa Explorers!

  • Look

    Peek at exclusive photos of the works and the artists.
